# Prevent Render Free Tier Sleep

Render free apps sleep after 15 minutes of inactivity.
UptimeRobot pings your app every 5 minutes — keeps it awake for free.

## Setup (2 minutes)

1. Go to https://uptimerobot.com → Sign up free
2. Click "Add New Monitor"
3. Monitor Type: HTTP(s)
4. Friendly Name: DGR Dashboard
5. URL: https://dgr-performance-dashboard.onrender.com
6. Monitoring Interval: Every 5 minutes
7. Click "Create Monitor"

Done — your app stays awake 24/7 at zero cost.
